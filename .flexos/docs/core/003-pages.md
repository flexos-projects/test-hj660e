This sitemap represents the ideal structure for the new ISC website, designed for intuitive navigation, clear audience segmentation, and maximum SEO impact.

### Top-Level Navigation

*   Products (Dropdown: BIND 9, Kea)
*   Services (For Enterprise)
*   Resources (For Community)
*   About Us
*   Knowledge Hub (Blog)
*   Contact

---

### Page Breakdown

**1. Homepage**
*   **Route:** `/`
*   **Title:** ISC: The Trusted Foundation of the Internet
*   **Meta:** The Internet Systems Consortium (ISC) develops and supports the world's most critical open-source internet software, including BIND 9 and Kea DHCP.
*   **Purpose:** To instantly communicate ISC's authority and cleanly segment the two primary audiences.
*   **Content Sections:**
    *   Hero section with the core value proposition: "The trusted, modern stewards of the internet's core infrastructure."
    *   Prominent dual-path CTA: "For the Community" and "For the Enterprise".
    *   "Our Impact in Numbers" block (e.g., stats on deployment, queries).
    *   Featured Products (BIND & Kea).
    *   Featured Enterprise Service (Support).
    *   Latest articles from the Knowledge Hub.
    *   Callout to their non-profit mission.

**2. Products > BIND 9**
*   **Route:** `/products/bind/`
*   **Title:** BIND 9: The World's Most Trusted DNS Software
*   **Meta:** Learn about, download, and get support for BIND 9, the open-source DNS software that powers a vast portion of the internet.
*   **Purpose:** A comprehensive overview for the world's standard DNS server.
*   **Content Sections:** Overview, Key Features & Benefits, Use Cases (Recursive vs. Authoritative), Prominent CTAs to "Download" and "View Documentation", Link to Enterprise Support.

**3. Products > Kea DHCP**
*   **Route:** `/products/kea/`
*   **Title:** Kea: A Modern, High-Performance DHCP Server
*   **Meta:** Discover Kea, the next-generation open-source DHCP server from ISC, designed for performance, flexibility, and reliability.
*   **Purpose:** Introduce and promote ISC's modern DHCP solution.
*   **Content Sections:** Similar structure to BIND 9 page, highlighting its modernity and advantages.

**4. Services (Enterprise Gateway)**
*   **Route:** `/enterprise/`
*   **Title:** Enterprise Services for Mission-Critical Infrastructure | ISC
*   **Meta:** Get expert support, training, and consulting for BIND 9 and Kea DHCP directly from the source. Ensure the stability and security of your network.
*   **Purpose:** A polished landing page for the Decision-Maker persona. It sells the "why" before the "what".
*   **Content Sections:** Hero emphasizing reliability and expertise, Overview of Services (Support, Training, Consulting), Client Testimonials/Logos, "Why ISC?" value propositions, primary CTA to "Request a Quote".
*   **Internal Linking:** This page links out to the detailed service pages below.

**5. Services > Enterprise Support**
*   **Route:** `/enterprise/support/`
*   **Title:** 24/7 BIND & Kea Support | ISC Enterprise Services
*   **Meta:** Protect your critical infrastructure with an ISC support contract. Get direct access to the engineers who write the software.
*   **Purpose:** Detail the support offerings and drive quote requests.
*   **Content Sections:** Detailed breakdown of support tiers, SLAs, benefits, access to experts, clear CTA.

**6. Resources (Community Gateway)**
*   **Route:** `/resources/`
*   **Title:** Technical Resources for BIND 9 & Kea DHCP | ISC
*   **Meta:** Access comprehensive documentation, software downloads, knowledge base articles, and community mailing lists for ISC software.
*   **Purpose:** The central hub for the Operator persona. Efficiency is key.
*   **Content Sections:** Massive, prominent search bar is the hero. Clear links to Documentation, Downloads, Mailing Lists, Knowledge Base.

**7. About Us**
*   **Route:** `/about/`
*   **Title:** About ISC: Stewards of Internet Infrastructure
*   **Meta:** Learn about the Internet Systems Consortium's history, our non-profit mission, and our decades-long commitment to a stable, open internet.
*   **Purpose:** Tell the "Unsung Architects" story and build institutional trust.
*   **Content Sections:** Our Mission (501c3 focus), "The Unsung Architects" narrative, interactive history timeline, link to the Impact and Experts pages.

**8. About Us > Our Experts**
*   **Route:** `/about/experts/`
*   **Title:** Our Experts | The ISC Team
*   **Meta:** Meet the world-class engineers and protocol experts behind BIND 9, Kea DHCP, and ISC's contributions to internet standards.
*   **Purpose:** Humanize the brand and showcase the incredible talent pool.
*   **Content Sections:** A grid of team members with professional photos, names, titles, and bios accessible via modals or individual pages.

**9. Knowledge Hub (Blog)**
*   **Route:** `/blog/`
*   **Title:** The ISC Knowledge Hub: Insights on Internet Infrastructure
*   **Meta:** Expert articles, technical deep dives, and announcements from the team at the Internet Systems Consortium.
*   **Purpose:** The home for thought leadership content.
*   **Content Sections:** A filterable list of articles, featuring the latest posts prominently.

**10. Contact**
*   **Route:** `/contact/`
*   **Title:** Contact The Internet Systems Consortium (ISC)
*   **Meta:** Get in touch with ISC for enterprise sales inquiries, general questions, or to report a security vulnerability.
*   **Purpose:** A clear, functional contact page.
*   **Content Sections:** Segmented contact info: Enterprise Sales (link to quote form), General Inquiries (simple form/email), Security Vulnerabilities (clear instructions/PGP key), Mailing Address.
