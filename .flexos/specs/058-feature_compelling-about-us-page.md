### Description

This feature is about crafting a narrative. The "About Us" page will tell the "Unsung Architects" story of ISC, moving beyond a dry corporate description. It will highlight the non-profit mission, the organization's pivotal history in building the internet, and its unwavering commitment to an open and stable internet. This page is foundational for building brand identity, trust, and emotional connection with both community and enterprise audiences.

### User Stories

-   **As a potential enterprise partner,** I want to understand the mission and history of the organization, so I can trust that I'm partnering with a stable, mission-driven entity.
-   **As a community member,** I want to be reminded of ISC's non-profit roots and commitment to the open internet, so I feel aligned with the organization's values.
-   **As a journalist,** I want a central place to find the official story, mission statement, and history of ISC, so I can accurately represent the organization in my writing.

### Acceptance Criteria

-   A dedicated "/about" page is created.
-   The page content clearly articulates ISC's non-profit mission.
-   The page includes a narrative section that tells the story of ISC's history and its role in the internet's development.
-   The content highlights the organization's core values and commitment to an open internet.
-   The page may include a high-level overview of the leadership team, with a link to the full "Our Experts" page.
-   The design and tone are professional yet passionate, reflecting the brand's unique position.
-   All text and media on the page are easily editable in the CMS.
-   The page is fully responsive and accessible.

### Technical Notes

-   This is primarily a content and design feature. The technical implementation is a standard content page.
-   Consider using compelling visuals, such as historical photos or custom graphics, to support the narrative. These assets should be optimized for the web.
-   The content for this page requires significant input from ISC leadership and the marketing team to ensure the story is told authentically and effectively.
